ZIMA RETURNS

Today marks a momentous occasion as we unveil our rebranding journey. With renewed purpose and commitment to excellence, we embrace change to better serve our valued patrons. Our revitalized identity reflects our growth, core values, and dedication to exceptional experiences. Join us in this exciting new chapter of possibilities.


At ZIMA, we believe that great taste and sophistication can go hand in hand. Our drinks are crafted to provide a unique and refreshing experience for adults who appreciate the finer things in life. Whether you're enjoying a night out with friends or relaxing at home, ZIMA is the perfect choice.

Origin Story

In the summer of 2019, a group of friends seeking respite from the scorching heat gathered at a cozy café in a small town. Bound by a shared passion for sophistication and fine tastes, they were dissatisfied with the mundane beverage options available. This dissatisfaction sparked an idea - an idea that would transform the world of refreshments forever.

Fueled by their vision, the friends embarked on a journey of exploration and innovation. Endless nights were spent experimenting with an array of ingredients, meticulously refining flavors to craft a masterpiece that would redefine the beverage industry. They sought guidance from experts, studied the nuances of flavor profiles, and embraced every challenge with unwavering dedication.


Months of research culminated in a defining moment - the birth of ZIMA. Named after the Russian word for "winter," ZIMA embodied the essence of cool and refreshing experiences that the friends had envisioned. It became an ode to their collective pursuit of a unique and sophisticated drinking experience. With a sense of pride and accomplishment, they introduced ZIMA to the world, and its distinctive flavors quickly captured the hearts of connoisseurs and casual drinkers alike.


Today, ZIMA stands as a testament to the power of friendship, passion, and perseverance. Its origin story serves as a reminder that greatness can emerge from humble beginnings. From those initial conversations at the café to the global sensation it has become, ZIMA's legacy of refreshment, sophistication, and the pursuit of excellence continues to inspire.
